WE ARE PRIMOS

Born in Portland, OR, Primos Cycles was founded by two bike enthusiasts who share a deep love for all things cycling.

Our Story

Primo Cycles was born from a simple passion: the love of cycling. Founded in Portland, Oregon, by two lifelong bike enthusiasts, we set out to create bicycles that combine innovation, performance, and timeless design.

Every bike we create is crafted with attention to detail, using the finest materials and cutting-edge technology to ensure the best riding experience for our customers. We believe that cycling is more than just transportation – it's a way of life.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Primo Cycles

Quality Craftsmanship

Every bike is meticulously crafted using premium materials and state-of-the-art technology to ensure exceptional performance and durability.

Passion for Cycling
